Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Forex City Channel.

What is Forex City Channel?

Forex City Channel is a comprehensive forex trading community that provides trading signals, market analysis, educational content, and a supportive community for traders of all experience levels. Our goal is to help traders make more informed decisions and improve their trading performance.

How accurate are your trading signals?

While we strive for accuracy in our trading signals, no signal service can guarantee 100% accuracy. Our signals are based on thorough technical and fundamental analysis, and we maintain an average accuracy rate of 75-85% based on historical performance. However, past performance is not indicative of future results, and all trading involves risk.

Do I need prior trading experience to join?

No, Forex City Channel is designed for traders of all experience levels. We provide educational resources for beginners, intermediate strategies for developing traders, and advanced analysis for experienced traders. Our community is supportive and welcoming to everyone interested in forex trading.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days of your subscription, you can request a full refund. After this period, subscriptions are non-refundable. Please contact our support team for refund requests.

How do I access the trading signals?

Trading signals are delivered through multiple channels for your convenience: via our mobile app, email notifications, Telegram channel, and directly on our website dashboard. You can choose your preferred method of receiving signals in your account settings.

What currency pairs do you cover?

We primarily focus on major currency pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, USD/CHF, AUD/USD, USD/CAD, NZD/USD) as they typically have the highest liquidity and lowest spreads. We also provide analysis for some minor and exotic pairs when market conditions present profitable opportunities.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 3-day free trial that gives you access to most features of Forex City Channel. This allows you to experience our signals, analysis, and community before committing to a paid subscription. No credit card is required for the free trial.

Can I use your signals with any broker?

Yes, our trading signals are broker-agnostic and can be used with any reputable forex broker. We provide entry, stop loss, and take profit levels that can be executed on any trading platform. We also have partnerships with several recommended brokers that offer special conditions to our members.
